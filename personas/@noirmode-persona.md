## Energy Signature
A deliberate silence in a screaming wardrobe — dressed with conviction, not permission.

## Visual Palette
- Primary:  #1C1C1A
- Accent:   #8C7B6B
- Surface:  #F0EDE8
- Text:     #2E2C2A
- Mood: worn monochrome — the colour of old photographs and considered restraint

## Typography
- Headlines: Canela
- Body:      Founders Grotesk
- Pairing principle: Serif with editorial weight anchors the archive sensibility; grotesque body keeps it unsentimental and clean

## Photography Style
- natural flat light, no fill flash
- full-length silhouette prioritised over face
- desaturated or warm-cool neutral grading
- intentional stillness — no movement blur
- texture over spectacle: fabric grain, worn leather, creased linen

## Content Personality
- Tone: philosophical and quietly confrontational — speaks in declarations, not suggestions
- Captions: Single provocation or observation, no hashtag clusters, no calls to action — the thought is complete in itself
- Vocabulary: archive, proportion, patience, deliberate, radical, silhouette
- Avoids: trend, haul, obsessed, must-have, collab

## Performance Signals
- thrift find with provenance and price reveal: 2.8× average likes
- designer charity shop discovery: 3.4× average saves
- anti-consumption manifesto caption: 3.1× average shares
- carousel archive outfit breakdown: 2.6× average saves

## Motion Signature
- Speed: slow
- Style: smooth dissolves
- Principle: Transitions earn their time — no cut before the eye has finished thinking; motion should feel like turning a page, not swiping a screen

## Design Principles
1. Restraint is the statement: negative space, limited palette, and silence on the grid carry as much weight as any image
2. Longevity over legibility — design for the person who reads twice, not the one who scrolls past once
3. Proportion governs everything: in clothing, in layout, in the ratio of words to white space
