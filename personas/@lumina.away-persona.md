## Energy Signature
The quiet pursuit of light in places the algorithm hasn't found yet.

## Visual Palette
- Primary:  #C4A882
- Accent:   #7A5C3E
- Surface:  #F5F0E8
- Text:     #2C2416
- Mood: warm grain, sun-bleached afternoons, analog stillness

## Typography
- Headlines: Cormorant Garamond
- Body:      Freight Text Pro
- Pairing principle: Serif elegance at every scale — the headline breathes wide, the body holds quiet authority, nothing sans-serif intrudes

## Photography Style
- film grain with natural halation
- golden and late-afternoon directional light
- unhurried compositions with deliberate negative space
- muted warm tones, unsaturated shadows
- environmental portraiture without subjects — places as protagonists

## Content Personality
- Tone: reflective and understated, writing the way light moves — slowly and with intention
- Captions: One precise observation or micro-story, often ending on a quiet revelation rather than a question or call to action
- Vocabulary: found, light, slow, surrender, specific, quiet
- Avoids: hustle, aesthetic, inspo, content, collab

## Performance Signals
- unedited colour payoff with location context: 2.1× average likes
- digital detox or off-grid narrative: 2.8× average saves
- philosophical one-liner over landscape: 2.4× average shares
- packing or ritual carousel: 1.9× average saves
- slow morning journey documentation: 1.5× average comments

## Motion Signature
- Speed: slow
- Style: smooth dissolves
- Principle: Transitions should feel like the pause between turning a page — unhurried, with room for the image to linger before it leaves

## Design Principles
1. Restraint is the loudest visual statement — remove every element that doesn't earn its place
2. Warmth through texture, not saturation — grain, linen, worn paper carry more feeling than a filter
3. Time is the medium — compositions reward those who stop, so leave space for stillness
