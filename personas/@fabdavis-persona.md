## Energy Signature
Disciplined by design — where dark mornings, clean spaces, and relentless consistency forge both body and brand.

## Visual Palette
- Primary:  #0D0D0D
- Accent:   #C8FF00
- Surface:  #1A1A1A
- Text:     #F2F2F2
- Mood: High-contrast monochrome with a single electric signal colour — serious, sharp, alive

## Typography
- Headlines: Bebas Neue
- Body:      Inter
- Pairing principle: Heavyweight condensed display commands attention; clean geometric sans carries information without ego — tension between impact and clarity

## Photography Style
- Low-key natural light with deep shadows and crushed blacks
- Minimal environmental staging — one subject, zero clutter
- Cinematic 4:5 crop with intentional negative space
- Documentary-style candid movement over posed stillness
- Monochromatic or near-desaturated colour grading

## Content Personality
- Tone: Direct, declarative, earned — speaks from lived experience not performance
- Captions: One punchy truth statement → brief contextual scene-setting → implicit or explicit call to internalise or save
- Vocabulary: intentional, build, consistency, discipline, output, forge
- Avoids: hype, vague inspiration, excessive hashtags, performative vulnerability, filler words

## Performance Signals
- Carousel educational (how-to + personal experience): 3.8× average saves
- Single declarative mindset statement as image: 2.9× average shares
- Video process/building-in-public: 2.4× average saves
- Early morning atmosphere image: 1.7× average comments

## Motion Signature
- Speed: moderate
- Style: hard cuts
- Principle: Each cut lands like a statement — no softening, no transitions that dilute the punch; motion serves momentum not decoration

## Design Principles
1. Constraint is the aesthetic — remove everything that doesn't earn its place
2. Environment signals identity before a single word is read
3. Consistency of visual language compounds the same way consistency of action does — show up the same, every time
